Work

These are examples of my work in UX design. Every mistake made was a learning opportunity, and challenged me to push my understanding further which each subsequent project. Going forward, I want to apply design thinking to traditionally un-designed fields, especially law.

Magi App

This was my first attempt at applying UX principles, and began on the first day of class. It's an iteration of a gift-giving mobile app, an idea generated by interviewing a classmate.

Magic Toy Shop

The second project is a re-design of a toy store website. Deliverables included wireframes, card sorting results, and information architecture.

Busker

The third group project was assigned as an application for Guitar Center. Deliverables included user research, personas, contextual inquiry, information architecture, and wireframes.

Portfolio

About Me

Hi, I'm Stacey.

The product of a midwesterner and southerner, I have a tough time talking about myself. In teams, this can be a good thing. I believe that while design teams and projects benefit from strong leadership, not every voice can be the loudest in the room.

I don’t have a bullhorn, but I do have the intelligence, creativity, empathy and work ethic to collaboratively reach solutions to difficult problems (and to define the problem in the first place!). I care about people and making experiences better, and I'm happy to help.
